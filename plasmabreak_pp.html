Privacy Policy for Plasma Break

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Plasma Break ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our mobile application (the "Game").

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you contact customer support. This may include your email address and the content of your message.

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you access or use our Game, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and your use of the Game, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Information: Details about how you use our Game, such as access times, hardware settings, and crash reports.

C. Information from Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. The services we use may include:

  • Google Play Services (for authentication, achievements, and leaderboards)
  • AdMob (Google) (for serving advertisements)
  • Firebase (for analytics, remote configuration, and crash reporting)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Game.
  • Develop new features and services.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.
  • Personalize your experience, including providing advertisements that may be of interest to you.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Game.

3. Advertising

We may work with third-party advertising partners (like AdMob) to serve ads in our Game. These partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and other technologies to collect information about your use of the Game and other apps to provide targeted advertisements.

If you purchase the "Remove Ads" option, we stop serving ads to your device, but we may still collect analytics data to improve the game performance.

4. In-App Purchases

Our Game offers in-app purchases (e.g., coins, skins, removing ads). All transactions are processed through the Google Play Store. We do not store your credit card or payment information on our servers.

5. Data Retention

We store the information we collect for as long as it is necessary for the purpose(s) for which we originally collected it. We may also retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our website or Game).
